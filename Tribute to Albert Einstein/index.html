Albert Einstein

He is considered the most important, well-known and popular scientist of the 20th century.

Albert Einstein in 1947

  • 14 / March / 1879: Born in Ulm, Germany.
  • 1905: I write several fundamental papers on small and large scale physics. In the first of them he explained the Brownian motion, in the second the photoelectric effect, and the remaining two developed special relativity and mass-energy equivalence.
  • 1908: He was hired at the University of Bern, Switzerland, as a professor and lecturer.
  • 1915: I present the theory of general relativity, in which I fully formulate the concept of gravity.
  • 16 / april / 1955: Died due to internal bleeding.
